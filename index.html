Community Outreaches

Building Your Life Ministries offers monthly grocery assistance to families, ensuring that basic necessities are met with compassion and care. In addition to this practical support, the church is deeply committed to spiritual growth and upliftment. Through various outreach programs—such as counseling, youth mentorship, and prayer meetings—the church seeks to strengthen individuals not only physically but also spiritually, fostering a sense of hope, faith, and belonging within the community.
